About This Platform

Why Phishing Awareness?

Over 90% of cyber attacks begin with a phishing email. Attackers exploit human psychology rather than technical vulnerabilities. This platform is designed to transform users from targets into the first line of defense.

Course Objectives

  • Identify key indicators of phishing and spoofing.
  • Understand the psychology behind social engineering.
  • Analyze URLs and verify website authenticity.
  • Implement cybersecurity best practices at home and work.
